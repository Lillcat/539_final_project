Positano

Nocelle

The view from the Path of the Gods, which begins in Bomerano and winds its way to Nocelle, was nothing short of divine, with endless stretches of sparkling azure waters, rugged cliffs, and quaint villages nestled along the Amalfi Coast. Each step revealed a new breathtaking vista, making the journey as awe-inspiring as the destination.

Path of the Gods

Hiking the Path of the Gods was a breathtaking adventure, where every step revealed sweeping views of the Amalfi Coast's azure waters and dramatic cliffs. The trail's beauty was matched only by its challenges, making each hard-earned vista feel like a triumph over the terrain—and a moment to savor forever.

Hotel Casa Albertina

The view of Positano from our balcony at Hotel Casa Albertina was nothing short of magical, with the town’s colorful cliffs glowing in the soft golden light. The faint sound of music drifted up from the bustling streets below, creating a perfect symphony to accompany the breathtaking sunset.

Pepè Positano

Shopping in Positano’s charming boutiques was a delight, especially discovering beautifully crafted purses that perfectly captured the town’s vibrant spirit. I couldn’t resist treating myself and my family to these one-of-a-kind treasures, each bag a stylish keepsake from this unforgettable coastal escape.

Arienzo Beach Club

Arienzo Beach Club offered a slice of paradise, accessible only by boat, adding a touch of adventure to the experience. With golden sands, crystal-clear waters, and the sound of waves as our backdrop, we lounged on sunbeds, sipped refreshing Aperol spritzes, and soaked in the luxury and natural beauty of Positano.

Amalfi Drive

The journey to Positano along the Amalfi Drive was both thrilling and unforgettable, with its narrow, winding roads hugging the cliffs high above the sea. While the sharp turns and sheer drops were nerve-wracking, the breathtaking views of the sparkling coastline and colorful villages below made every moment worth it.
