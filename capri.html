Capri

Sina Flora

We stayed at the most beautiful and quaint boutique hotel, Sina Flora, nestled in the heart of Capri. Its perfect location allowed us to easily explore the island’s highlights, while the hotel itself offered charming terraces, stunning views, and an intimate ambiance that made it feel like a true home away from home.

Faraglioni Bar

The Faraglioni Bar at Sina Flora was an enchanting retreat, where we enjoyed a delightful aperitivo in the hotel’s lush garden terrace. Sipping on expertly crafted cocktails surrounded by vibrant blooms and overlooking the serene Capri landscape was the perfect way to unwind and savor the island’s elegance.

Lo Scoglio Delle Sirene Beach Club

Lo Scoglio Delle Sirene Beach Club was a serene escape, where we enjoyed delicious pizza and refreshing Aperol spritzes on its stunning rocky beach. With the sound of gentle waves and the breathtaking Capri coastline as our backdrop, it was the perfect blend of relaxation and indulgence.

Gardens of Augustus

The Gardens of Augustus were a breathtaking oasis, offering panoramic views of Capri’s dramatic cliffs, the iconic Faraglioni rocks, and the shimmering Tyrrhenian Sea. Strolling through the vibrant, manicured gardens felt like stepping into a postcard, with every corner revealing another picture-perfect scene.

Coast of Capri

The crystal-clear waters of Capri were absolutely mesmerizing, and swimming in them during our private boat tour was pure magic. Surrounded by towering cliffs and the gentle rhythm of the waves, it was an unforgettable moment of bliss shared with my boyfriend.

Boat Tour of Capri

Our boat tour around the island of Capri was the highlight of our trip, offering an unparalleled way to experience the island’s breathtaking beauty. From gliding through sparkling turquoise waters to discovering hidden grottos and dramatic cliffs, every moment felt like a dream—and the memories we made on that boat are ones we’ll cherish forever.
