Rome

L'Antico Forno di Piazza Trevi

Sipping cappuccinos at a charming café in front of the Trevi Fountain was a quintessential Roman experience. With the fountain’s cascading waters as our backdrop, we soaked in the vibrant atmosphere while enjoying the perfect blend of history, culture, and coffee.

Colosseum

Standing before the Colosseum in Rome was like stepping into a living piece of history, its grandeur and scale leaving us in awe. Exploring the ancient amphitheater, we could almost hear the echoes of its storied past, making it a truly unforgettable experience.

Roman Forum

Wandering through the Roman Forum felt like traveling back in time, surrounded by the beautifully preserved ruins of ancient temples and arches. The lush greenery and blooming flowers that dotted the grounds added a serene charm, making it a breathtaking blend of history and natural beauty.

Oro Bistrot

Oro Bistrot in Rome offered an unforgettable experience with its stunning rooftop view of the city at sunset. Paired with a perfectly crafted aperitivo, it was the ideal spot to unwind and take in the magic of Rome’s skyline.

Gardens of Villa Borghese

The Gardens of Villa Borghese were a peaceful escape from the bustle of Rome, with sprawling green lawns, elegant sculptures, and picturesque pathways. Wandering through the serene oasis, we felt a world away, surrounded by the timeless beauty of nature and art.

Barnum Café

Barnum Café was a cozy oasis in the heart of Rome, serving the best breakfast we had on our entire trip. With rich coffee, flaky pastries, and a charming patio to soak in the city’s atmosphere, it was the perfect way to start our day in true Roman style.

Vatican City

Visiting the Vatican was an awe-inspiring experience, where the grandeur of St. Peter’s Basilica and the artistry of the Sistine Chapel left us speechless. Surrounded by centuries of history and culture, every moment felt like a spiritual and artistic journey.

La Chiesa di Sant’Ignazio di Loyola

La Chiesa di Sant’Ignazio di Loyola was a hidden gem, with its breathtaking ceiling frescoes creating the illusion of a soaring dome. Standing beneath the masterpiece, we marveled at the artistry and detail that made this church an unforgettable highlight of Rome.

Tonnarello

The carbonara at Tonnarello in Trastevere was a revelation, with its creamy, velvety sauce and perfectly cooked pasta that melted in your mouth. Every bite was a testament to the simplicity and perfection of authentic Roman cuisine.

Piazza Navona

Piazza Navona was a lively masterpiece, with its stunning fountains, historic architecture, and vibrant street performers creating an unforgettable atmosphere. Strolling through the square, we felt immersed in Rome’s charm, surrounded by art, culture, and the energy of the city.

Trevi Fountain

The Trevi Fountain at night was pure magic, illuminated in soft golden light that made its intricate sculptures glow against the dark sky. The sound of cascading water and the romantic atmosphere created an unforgettable moment as we tossed coins and made our wishes.

Prati

Dancing in the streets of Prati, Rome, to the soulful melodies of a violinist was a moment of pure magic. Under the glow of the city lights, we let the music sweep us away, creating a memory that felt like it belonged in a romantic film.
