Kate's Trip to Italy

This summer, I embarked on an unforgettable journey through Southern Italy, immersing myself in its rich history, vibrant culture, and awe-inspiring landscapes. From savoring authentic carbonara in Rome to conquering the Path of the Gods in Positano, every experience was a story waiting to be told. Whether marveling at the ruins of Pompeii or indulging in sun-drenched bliss on Capri, I uncovered the soul of la dolce vita—one breathtaking moment at a time. I hope you enjoy this journey as much as I did.

  1. May 21, 2024

    Rome, Italy

    Rome is the capital of Italy and a city where history and modernity collide, offering everything from ancient wonders like the Colosseum and Vatican to charming cobblestone streets lined with gelaterias.

    Learn more
  2. May 25, 2024

    Pompeii, Italy

    Our brief stop in Pompeii offered a captivating glimpse into ancient history, with its remarkably preserved ruins bringing the past vividly to life.

  3. May 25, 2024

    Positano, Italy

    Positano is a picturesque town on the Amalfi Coast, where vibrant cliffside homes cascade down to crystal-clear waters. From hiking the stunning Path of the Gods to indulging in fresh seafood and aperitifs at beachfront cafes, every moment felt like a postcard come to life.

    Learn more
  4. May 28, 2024

    Sorrento, Italy

    Sorrento captivated us with its coastal charm, from a delightful wine tasting and olive oil tour to a seaview cooking class, where we savored the rich flavors of the region’s finest local produce.

  5. May 29, 2024

    Capri, Italy

    Capri is a glamorous island escape, where sparkling turquoise waters meet dramatic cliffs and luxury abounds. Between lounging at beach clubs, exploring the Gardens of Augustus, and savoring fine dining with breathtaking views, the island’s timeless elegance left us in awe.
